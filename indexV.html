OUR STORY

"Rasna" is a journey of passion, flavors, and a deep-rooted love for culinary artistry. Our story, which was founded in 1990, started with a straightforward ambition: to provide an out-of-the-ordinary dining experience. Our founder Aindya Sundar Dey, had aspired to blend innovation and tradition by utilising locally sourced, fresh ingredients to create delicacies that would be remembered. Over the years, we have grown into a beloved dining destination, cherished by residents and tourist. We have continuously tried to evolve and improved our menu by introducing new flavours, expanding our selection, and adding fresh new elements.We are immensely grateful for our supporters and the trust that our beloved guests have placed in us as their dining destination.
